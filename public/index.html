Freshtag is the easiest and fastest way to video chat. There's no client and no login. Just type in a hashtag to join or create a session.

Your FreshTag nick-name is completely anonymous, so have fun with it! Find a conversation, start a meeting, ask a question, maybe find a new friend, and above all, always keep it 100.
